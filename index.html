About Us

The ‘BOOKshelf’ is a platform where used textbooks can be provided to the under privileged children and students at fairly cheaper rates. Our idea is to employ this platform, where we can make textbooks and stationery available for those who are not able to spend large amounts of money on books and education. The goal is to be able to provide the high quality educational material to every one regardless of their social, geographical and economic status. Donation centres will be established in schools, colleges along with this website for books collection and sale. Supply networks will have connection with different NGOs and similar organizations to identify more and more students who are in need of our services. Thus it is a small step towards shaping a better future working together.
